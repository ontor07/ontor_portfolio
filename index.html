About Me

I Am Alauddin Ontor

I'm Ontor, a video editor with a deep passion for visual storytelling, shaped by a multi-disciplinary creative journey. I began in 2017 by learning graphic design, which laid the foundation for my design thinking and aesthetic sense. In 2020, I expanded into web design and development, gaining technical fluency and a broader understanding of digital platforms.

From 2022 to 2024, I worked in the skill-based IT sector, where I honed my ability to bridge creativity and technology. But it was during this time that I realized my true passion lies in video editing—where emotion, rhythm, and visuals come together to tell powerful stories.

I spent a year working at MAT Corporation as a video editor, creating brand-aligned content, social media visuals, and emotionally resonant narratives. Now, I’ve transitioned into full-time freelancing, where I focus on crafting audience-centric video content that blends clarity, emotion, and purpose.

My goal is simple: to make every cut, transition, and sound serve the story.
